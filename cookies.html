Our Use of Cookies

Last updated: March 21, 2026 — At Oak Moss World ("we," "us," or "our"), we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our verdant digital platform. This Cookies Policy explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use. Our commitment to ecological mindfulness extends to your digital footprint.

78,900+ words of gardening wisdom — served with care for your privacy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you navigate our vast library of plant guides, landscape articles, and organic workshops. Think of them as friendly pollinators — they help things run smoothly while respecting the ecosystem of your data.

We use both first-party cookies (set by Oak Moss World) and third-party cookies (from trusted partners like analytics and video platforms) to improve functionality and content relevance.

Types of Cookies We Use

To make our content (over 124 detailed gardening guides) accessible and personalized, we classify cookies in the following categories:

Cookie CategoryPurposeExamples & Duration
Essential (Strictly Necessary)Enable core functionality: page navigation, secure areas, form submissions (like our contact form). Without these, the site cannot operate properly.Session cookies, login state, CSRF tokens. Expire after browser close or up to 1 day.
Preference & FunctionalityRemember your choices (language, region, favorite garden topics) to offer a personalized experience."remember_me", preferred content type. Duration: up to 12 months.
Analytics & PerformanceHelp us understand how visitors engage with our 78k+ word library — which articles are most loved, how you navigate workshops. We use aggregated data to improve content.Google Analytics (anonymized IP), custom event trackers. Duration: up to 24 months.
Marketing & Social MediaAllow us to measure effectiveness of green campaigns and offer social sharing features. We never sell personal data.Social sharing cookies (YouTube, Instagram widgets). Duration: session to persistent.

We do not use cookies for intrusive cross-site advertising. Any third-party tools respect current privacy regulations (GDPR / CCPA / LGPD).

Why We Use Cookies — Our Verdant Philosophy

  • 🌿 Enhance Your Journey: Remember your favorite plant families, reading progress, or saved articles.
  • 📊 Improve Our Content Ecosystem: Understand which gardening topics (native plants, permaculture, soil health) resonate most.
  • 🌼 Provide Seamless Workshops & Event Registration: Cookies help us manage availability and prevent duplicate form submissions.
  • 🔒 Maintain Security & Integrity: Protect against fraudulent activity and keep the Oak Moss community safe.

Your Control & Consent Choices

You are in the gardener's seat. When you first visit Oak Moss World, you will see a cookie banner that lets you accept, reject, or customize cookie categories (except essential ones). You can modify your preferences at any time through our Cookie Preferences Center (accessible via the floating leaf icon or in the footer).

🍃 Manage your cookie settings anytime — because your digital garden should feel as natural as the outdoors.

Alternatively, most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through settings. However, disabling essential cookies may impact core functionality like submitting inquiries via the contact form. Reach out if you have questions.

Third-Party Services & Embedded Content

Our articles and guides may include embedded videos (YouTube), Instagram plant tours, or maps. These services may set their own cookies. We recommend reviewing their policies:

  • YouTube (Google) — Google Cookie Policy
  • Instagram (Meta) — Instagram Cookie Info
  • Google Analytics — anonymized tracking to count visits and understand popular garden content.

We also embed sign-up forms for our "Verdant Collective" newsletter; those are managed with Mailchimp, which uses analytics cookies only after explicit consent.

Retention & Updates to this Policy

Cookie lifespans vary based on type (session to 2 years). We review this Cookies Policy regularly to align with updated regulations and our evolving garden community. The effective date is shown at the top. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via website notice or email (if you are subscribed).

Questions? Let's Talk

If you have any doubts about our use of cookies, your data, or wish to withdraw consent, please contact our Data Steward (we call them the "Head of Digital Ecology").
